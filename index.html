Different types of c7 Corvettes

The Stingray Corvette

  • The Corvette Stingray is deisged to have a agressive front end.
  • The Stingry has a Carbon fiber hood, mirrors, and underbody panels.
  • The Base 6.2L LT1 V8 with direct injection,active fuel management, and variable valve timing; making about 450–455 hp.

The Grand Sport Corvette

  • Engine: 6.2L LT1 naturally aspirated V8 (460 hp, 465 lb-ft)
  • 0–60 mph: ~3.6 seconds and only available in 12 standard colors plus exclusive options like.
  • The Transmission only comes in 7-speed manual or 8-speed automatic.

The Z06 Corvette

  • The z06 Corvette is a high-performance sports car featuring a 6.2L supercharged LT4 V8 engine producing 650 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque.As the last front-engine Z06, it achieves 0-60 mph in 2.95 seconds, features extensive aerodynamics, and was available with a 7-speed manual or 8-speed automatic transmission.
  • The C7 Z06 is wider than the standard Stingray, with specific fender flares to house 285 (front) and 335 (rear) width tires. It also features a removable carbon fiber roof panel.
  • The Z06 features standard 6-piston front/4-piston rear Brembo brakes, with available carbon ceramic rotors, and Magnetorheological suspension for precise control.
The Zr1 Corvette
  • The zr1 corvette features a unique 755-horsepower 6.2L LT5 supercharged V8 engine that produces so much power and torque.
  • With a top speed over 212 mph and 0–60 mph in roughly 3 seconds, it is known to outperform cars costing upwards of $1 million.
  • Only 2,953 units were produced, all in 2019, with only about 30% equipped with a manual transmission.
